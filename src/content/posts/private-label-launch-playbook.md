Private label is the fastest path from "I want to sell something" to "I have a brand on the shelf." Here's the 90-day plan.

## Days 1–14: Category selection

Pick a category where: (1) you can hit USD 8–15 retail with healthy margin, (2) demand is proven on Amazon / Jumia / your channel, (3) the product is not a regulatory minefield (skip cosmetics and supplements on launch one).

## Days 15–30: Supplier shortlist & samples

Shortlist 5 ODM suppliers. Order paid samples from the top 3. Inspect against a written spec sheet — even a one-pager.

## Days 31–60: Packaging & compliance

Brand identity, packaging artwork, barcode registration, and any required compliance (FDA, NAFDAC, CE, RoHS). Compliance testing eats 2–4 weeks; start it the day the sample is approved.

## Days 61–75: PO, deposit, production

Lock the PO with payment terms 30/70 against B/L copy. Stay in weekly contact during production; silence is a red flag.

## Days 76–90: Pre-shipment inspection & shipment

Book the PSI. Resolve defects before the balance leaves your account. Ship.

The whole thing runs USD 8,000–25,000 in stock plus USD 1,500–4,000 in tooling, packaging, and compliance for a credible launch SKU.
